General Questions

What should I bring to my appointment?

We provide all the necessary amenities, such as towels and robes. However, if you have any specific needs or preferences, feel free to bring your own items, such as comfortable clothing.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to allow for a smooth check-in process and to help you relax before your treatment begins.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment time may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Service-Related Questions

What types of massages do you offer?

We offer a variety of massages, including Swedish, deep tissue, hot stone, and aromatherapy massages. Each massage is tailored to your individual needs.

Do you have services for couples?

Yes, we offer special packages for couples, including side-by-side massages and facials. Please inquire about availability when booking your appointment.

What products do you use for facials?

We use high-quality, natural skincare products that are suitable for all skin types. Our specialists will choose the best products for your skin's needs.

Payment and Booking Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and mobile payments. We do not accept checks.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. You can purchase them online or at our front desk.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our website, by phone, or by visiting us in person. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time.
